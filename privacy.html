How this website handles privacy

This page explains how Smart Home Automation handles browsing data, cookies, affiliate interactions, and general usage information on the website.

Overview

What we collect

Browsing behavior

We may understand how users browse pages so the experience can be improved over time.

Device and technical data

Standard technical details such as browser type or device size may be used for performance and layout optimization.

Affiliate clicks

When a user clicks an affiliate link, the destination merchant may collect data under its own privacy policy.

Cookies

Cookies or similar technologies may be used for theme preferences, compare lists, and site functionality.

Usage

How the information is used

Site improvement

Data can help improve search, filters, browsing flows, and page layout decisions.

Preference storage

Basic settings such as theme and compare selections may be stored locally in the browser.

Performance checks

Technical insights may be used to keep the site fast, stable, and easier to maintain.

Content accuracy

Feedback can help correct product data, descriptions, links, and SEO content on item pages.

User rights

What users can do

Clear browser data

Users can clear browser storage if they want to remove saved preferences or compare data.

Review merchant policies

Users should check the destination store’s privacy policy before completing a purchase.

Send feedback

Users can contact the team for privacy questions, corrections, or data-related concerns.

Limit cookies

Browser settings can usually be adjusted to restrict or remove cookies and similar storage.

Privacy by design

This website is built to keep the public browsing experience simple while using only the minimum practical data needed for product discovery, comparison, and affiliate navigation.
